## 📖 About The Project

Rapid urbanization has positioned air pollution as one of the most severe public health crises of the 21st century. The core problem is an information gap: citizens need to know not just raw scientific numbers, but what those numbers mean for their daily lives. 

This project bridges that gap by engineering a highly accessible, real-time **AQI Visualization Command Center** that translates complex atmospheric data into clear visual warnings and actionable lifestyle advice. Built as the CA1 Assignment for Environmental Studies (CHE110).

---

## ✨ Interactive Features 
*(Click the arrows below to expand)*

<details>
<summary><strong>🔴 Live Telemetry Hub & GPS Engine</strong></summary>
<br>
Uses GPS reverse-geocoding to fetch your exact local Air Quality Index. The entire UI dynamically shifts its theme from Safe (Glowing Green) to Hazardous (Glowing Red) based on the threat level.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>📊 Deep Diagnostics & Analytics</strong></summary>
<br>
A comprehensive 10-pollutant telemetry grid tracking PM2.5, PM10, NO2, O3, SO2, CO, and simulated historical constraints. Includes live Bar Charts and Origin Source Pie Charts.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>🌦️ Predictive Forecast Hub</strong></summary>
<br>
Maps out a 72-hour predictive AQI line chart paired with an algorithmic <strong>Lifestyle Matrix</strong> that actively advises you on whether to exercise outdoors, open your windows, or wear an N95 mask.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>⏳ The Temporal Roadmap</strong></summary>
<br>
An interactive, branching timeline visualizing India's atmospheric history from 2011 to projected sustainable or dystopian outcomes in 2041.
</details>
